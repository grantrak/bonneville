Denver

Denver boasts about its 300 days of sunshine a year. But it’s not only that it shines, but also what it shines upon. Nestled near the base of the Rocky Mountains, Denver offers panoramic views of what we call the Front Range, 140 miles of snowcapped peaks. You can see 200 peaks from the city, including 32 peaks that reach 13,000 feet or higher. It’s a sunny, dry climate with a towering mountain range off to the west.

Bonneville Denver relishes the fact that Denver is one of only a handful of cities with seven professional sports franchises, providing a bevy of quality content for our Sports Radio 104.3 The Fan and ESPN Denver 1600 stations. And if that doesn’t satiate appetites for entertainment, Denver offers 85 miles of bike paths, 90 golf courses, horse racing, professional rodeo, and a great city parks system. Bonneville Denver is also home to KOSI 101.1 Denver’s Real Music Variety Station and 98.5 KYGO Denver’s #1 for New Country.
