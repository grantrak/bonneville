Sacramento

Bonneville Sacramento stations are located mere minutes from DOCO the cities revitalized Downtown Commons. The California State Capitol is the centerpiece surrounded by a burgeoning local farm to fork restaurant scene, aptly making Sacramento, America’s Farm to Fork Capitol. Sacramento has long been touted as an agricultural mecca producing 80% of the world’s almonds, 33% of the world’s processed tomatoes, as well as supplying rice to Asian countries, as the 2nd largest producer of rice in the nation. Sacramento added a sparkling new arena, Golden One Center in 2016, which houses “Our Very Own Sacramento Kings”, and brings A-1 talent to the venue, making Sacramento a destination stop for an ever growing region. Sacramento is known for its close proximity to the Bay Area, the renowned Napa Valley, and unparalleled skiing in Lake Tahoe and the surrounding Sierra Nevada’s.

At Bonneville Sacramento, home of MIX 96, NOW 100.5, New Country 105.1 KNCI, and Sports 1140 KHTK our mission is thoughtful, informative, and positive programming as well as a personal touch with our audience through family friendly events that we produce including: “Kidtopia”, “Country in the Park”, “Pet-a-Palooza”, as well as the production of other concerts that benefit several charities in including: St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital and Cure Breast Cancer Inc. Sports 1140 KHTK is the flagship station for the NBA’s “Sacramento Kings” and carries play-by-play for “Oakland Raiders”, “Oakland A’s”, “Sacramento Republic”, “UC Davis Aggies”, and various NFL and College sports.
