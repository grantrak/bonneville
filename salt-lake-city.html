Salt Lake City

Salt Lake City is home to Bonneville corporate offices and is known for its affordability and robust economy, with easy access to a range of outdoor destinations. Salt Lake sits in a mountain bowl 4,300 feet up, with immediate access to all forms of adventure, from four world-class ski resorts 45 minutes from Salt Lake International Airport (with 10 more outside city limits) to a lineup of canyons to the east with a hundred options for hiking, biking, climbing and camping. Downtown, you can explore historic Temple Square located in the heart of downtown along with the headquarters of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Salt Lake is less than a day’s drive to any of the five national parks located in Utah: Arches, Canyonlands, Capitol Reef, Bryce and Zion.

In addition to hosting Bonneville corporate offices, our footprint in Salt Lake includes three iconic radio stations: KSL NewsRadio, FM100.3 and 103.5 The Arrow, one phenomenal TV station – local NBC affiliate KSL 5, with companion website ksl.com. Our integrated multi-platform newsroom is one of a kind, with a team providing the highest quality news content for radio, TV, web and print outlets. Every day across all our properties, we provide accurate news, compelling talk, and great music for our audience in a way that fulfills our mission to build up, connect, inform and celebrate Utah’s communities and families.
