San Francisco

In San Francisco, Bonneville serves one of the world’s most dynamic and attractive communities. The Bay Area ranks as the 4th largest metropolitan marketplace in the U.S., with rich cultural diversity and stunning natural beauty. San Francisco is a global center for high technology, biotechnology, medical research and finance, and includes a large support infrastructure of professional services including law, public relations, architecture and design. Tourists from around the world flock to the Bay Area to visit the famous Northern California coast, the California wine country, and to see the city’s famous landmarks including the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz, Fisherman’s Wharf, and Chinatown.

Bonneville San Francisco consists of four FM radio stations, each of which holds a special place in the Bay Area community. 96.5 KOIT is an iconic local brand, the area’s Adult Contemporary leader for generations. 99.7 NOW (KMVQ) is a cutting-edge outlet Contemporary Hit music, featuring high-profile personalities including Big Bay Morning hosts, Bennie and Greg, and St. John afternoons. 102.9 KBLX serves as “Throwbacks and R&B” a beacon to the diverse, urban East Bay community for four decades. And 98.5 KFOX is the South Bay’s home for classic rock.
