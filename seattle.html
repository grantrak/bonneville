Seattle

Bonneville’s Seattle stations are located near one of the fastest growing urban neighborhoods on the shores of Lake Union. Bonneville is proud to operate in an area home to both many small, local businesses as well as industry leaders such as Amazon, Starbucks, and Microsoft. Despite the myth that it rains all the time, locals rarely leave because of the natural outdoor environment that offers some of the best of hiking, biking and boating opportunities in the region. The city also offers majestic views of Mt. Rainier, Cascades and the bright blue waterways of the Puget Sound, which is home to the largest ferry system in the U.S. carrying more than 23 million passengers each year. Bonneville is proud to operate in a city home to iconic landmarks such as Pike Place Market, historic Pioneer Square, and of course, the Space Needle.

At Bonneville Seattle, home of KIRO Radio 97.3 FM, 710 ESPN Seattle, 770 KTTH, and MyNorthwest.com, our mission is connection. We connect entertainment to value; we connect discussion to understanding; and we connect people into community, enriching lives throughout the Pacific Northwest through news, sports, and community conversation. Bonneville’s sports stations also provide breaking news about the Seattle Seahawks located at the CenturyLink Field and the Seattle Mariners at Safeco Field.
